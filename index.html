Silence in Existence

Silence in Existence represents a unique collaboration between Ryan Somarathne and Mike De Zilva, blending their distinct sounds into a cohesive atmospheric experience.

It's an exploration of space, sound, and the silence that lives between notes.

1. The Void

A journey into the spaces we leave behind, the echoes that remain when the music stops.

2. Sonic Architecture

Building atmospheres that feel tangible, using sound to craft environments the listener can inhabit.

A sonic exploration of the space between.
